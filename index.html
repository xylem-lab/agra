About XylemLab

Advancing Agricultural Intelligence Through Remote Sensing

XylemLab at the University of Maryland develops cutting-edge remote sensing and machine learning solutions for agricultural monitoring and food security analysis across Africa.

Our Regional Food Balance Sheet provides critical intelligence for policymakers, researchers, and agricultural stakeholders by delivering timely, accurate crop yield forecasts using satellite data and advanced predictive models.

  • Monthly yield forecasts for maize, rice, and beans
  • Coverage across Tanzania, Malawi, Zambia, and Rwanda
  • Integration of NDVI, ESI, precipitation, and soil moisture data
  • Comparison with USDA and FAO estimates
  • Regional condition monitoring and early warning systems
